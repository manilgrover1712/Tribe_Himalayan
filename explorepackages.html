CHANDRAKHANI PASS

CHANDERKHANI PASS is a pass connecting Upper Kullu Valley with the ancient village of MALANA and Parvati Valley. It is at an elevation of about 3700m. It offers one of the best views in the whole valley. All the highest mountains can be seen from here. Indrasan 6221 m, Deo tibba 6001m, Inderkilla and many other high peaks can be seen from here. The picture above shows the mighty DHARAMSURA and PAPSURA 6400m.

HAMPTA PASS

Hampta Pass Trek is a trek which emerges through the lush green meadows of the upper Kullu Valley to the chilly barren cold desert of Spiti. The meadows, mountains, the snow glaciers, along with the streams of fresh white water adds to the eyefulness of the trek. 20 kms of dramatic scenery, constantly elevating altitude (from 2800m to 4300m highest), adventure of various kind is associated with this 2-3 days long trek..

BIJALI MAHADEV

BIJLI means lightning and MAHADEV is Shiva (Hindu God). In this temple of lightning, it is believed that the tall staff (60ft) attracts the lightning which falls on the temple and partially destroys the Shiv Linga inside of the temple. The priest of the temple has to restore the Shiva linga placed inside the temple using butter after every lightning as it shatters to pieces with flash of lightning. It is located at an altitude of about 2,500m in the Kullu Valley at about 20km from Kullu City. It can be approached by a trek of 3 km and 15-16 km journey by vehicle. The 3km trek also contains some very beautiful views. One needs to cross through thick jungle to reach the top of the mountain where the temple is situated.
